About

Hello Traveler, having a hard time reading subtitles? You can fix your subtitle problems on this website by making your own!


How to customize

  1. Head over to the Customize Subtitle Page to start customizing font, size, color, and transparency of your subtitles.
  2. Than, press the save button to save your settings. You will now see your settings in the text area, where you can type whatever you want to test out.
  3. Download your setting by pressing the Download button
  4. Lastly, add your new customize subtitle setting to a game file
